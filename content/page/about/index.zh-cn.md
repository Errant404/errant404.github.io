## 用户名含义

### errant

> 以下内容引自 [Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/errant)
>
> #### Adjective
>
> **errant** (*[comparative](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#comparative)* **[more](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/more#English) errant**, *[superlative](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#superlative)* **[most](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/most#English) errant**)
>
> 1. [Straying](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/stray#Verb) from the [proper](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/proper#Adjective) [course](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/course#Noun) or [standard](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/standard#Noun), or [outside](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/outside#Preposition) [established](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/established#Adjective) [limits](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/limit#Noun). 
>
> 2. [Roving](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/rove#Verb) around; [wandering](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/wandering#Adjective).
>
> 3. [Prone](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/prone#Adjective) to [making](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/make#Verb) [errors](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/error); [misbehaving](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/misbehaving#Adjective).
>
>    *We ran down the street in pursuit of the **errant** dog.*
>    
> 4. (*chiefly with a negative connotation, [obsolete](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#obsolete)*) *Obsolete form of \*[arrant](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/arrant#English)\* (“[complete](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/complete#Adjective); [downright](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/downright#Adjective), [utter](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/utter#Adjective)”)*. 
>
> #### Noun
>
> **errant** (*plural* **[errants](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/errants#English)**)
>
> 1. A [knight-errant](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/knight-errant).
